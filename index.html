Who is Blagica?

My name is Blagica Chadamova, a 20-year-old student at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FINKI) in Skopje. Originally from Kavadarci, I combine my passion for technology with strong leadership and creative skills.

Throughout my academic journey at Goce Delchev Primary School and Dobri Daskalov Gymnasium, I've demonstrated excellence in my studies and won awards for my business plan in Business & Management. My public speaking experience includes delivering speeches at my municipality, city library, and high school.

I thrive in team environments and excel under pressure, bringing strong organizational and leadership qualities to every project. My ability to plan effectively and communicate clearly makes me a valuable asset in both academic and professional settings.

Beyond academics, I'm passionate about dancing, drawing, and exploring new cultures through travel. My experiences in Italy and France have broadened my perspective and enhanced my creativity.

Driven by curiosity and a commitment to personal growth, I approach every challenge with focus and dedication. Whether it's a technical project or creative endeavor, I bring professionalism and attention to detail to all my work.
